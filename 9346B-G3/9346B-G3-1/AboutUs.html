About Us

Our Mission

To raise awareness and promote Human Rights in Mines View

Rights to Freedom Of Expression

Our team believes that everyone deserves to express themselves freely. Voicing out their ideas, their opinions, their talents.

Rights to Information

Our team believes that it is everyone's right to information. Information that is easily accessed for everyone. To know what is happening in today's society whether it be world events, big, and small.

Right to Belong

Our team believes in the saying "no man is an island" and it is your right to belong to a community and you have the right to cooperate with the community whatever condition you are in.

Who are we?

Our team consists of students from SLU taking up BSIT. Here, we wanted to the people of Mines View to be aware of the rights they have. We see that sometimes people don't really fully utilize the rights they have and we are here to change that.
