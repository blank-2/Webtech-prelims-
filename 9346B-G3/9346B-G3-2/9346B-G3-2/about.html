Gender Equality: "Stop judging each other instead start accepting each other"

Mission

Our mission is to support gender equality in and through the media, and in every aspect of administration, create strategies and activity intends to guarantee that gender equality is accomplished, forms the limit of women, men and all residents to be involve in procedures that will implement equality and equity.

This mission can be accomplished through creating activity designs in participative ways that unite people at work and make new pathways to determine worldwide issues by starting essential discussions to make opportunities and removing problems for men and women; creating support over all areas for programs that implement the reason for worldwide equality.

Vision

We envision a society with an ideal methods pertaining to physical, economic, educational, and social systems to raise the growth and success of all its members. We will provide programs that develop strengths, skills, and self-sufficiency in men and women and help them make meaningful choices in their lives with less conflict and more community support. We will undertake organizing campaigns to achieve safety and equality in the social, political, educational, athletic, economic, health, and media worlds of the smaller and larger communities in which men and women live and work.
