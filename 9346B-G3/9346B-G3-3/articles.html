Did you know?

  Mines View Park itself is a cultural heritage. Almost all thing we see in Mines View are related to cultural heritage. "Tapis" and "Kambal" (a traditional clothing of Igorot), Bul-ul figurines and other handicrafts created by the people of Mines View were inherited from their ancestor and were preserved until now. Cultural Heritages in Mines View Park are very important to the people of Mines View Park because it is the main source of their livelihood. It also helps not only the Mines View Park but also the entire Baguio City to boost the tourism. That's why as a citezen of Baguio City, a as a student, as a tourist, as a Filipino, it is our responsibility to protect our cultural heritage, to preserve our cultural heritage and save our cultural heritage!

Why do we need Protect, Preserve and Save our Cultural Heritage in Mines View Park?

  There are may reasons why do we need to do this for our cultural heritage. The reasons are:

  • It is the main source of the livelihood of the people of Mines View Park so by protecting it, we can help the residence of Mines View Park to earn income as well as the barangay officials so that they can improve and make the Mines View Park better.
  • It is a treasure.It is a legacy came from our ancestor through their sacrifices to protect and it so we must be the same.
  • It represents the history of Mines View Park and its identity.
  • We get a lot of benifits from here such as income, valuable things, livelihood, good memories and a lot more.

What you can do?

  • Follow the rules, regulations or instruction given by the authority of Mines View Park.
  • Respect the culture and traditions of the residence in Mines View Park.
  • Do not destroy, vandalize or do anything that will harm the Cultural Heritage of Mines View Park.
  • Support any activities of the authority in Mines View Park that will help the cultural heritages in Mines View.
  • Support the products created by the residence of Mines View Park.
  • Be proud in our cultural heritage.
