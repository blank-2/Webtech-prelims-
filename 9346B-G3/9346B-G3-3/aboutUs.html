About Us

We are Bachelor of Science in Information Technology students of Saint Louis University. Our goal is to raise awareness about the preservation and promotion of cultural heritages located in Mines View, Baguio City.

Goal

Cultural Heritage is the legacy that our ancestors left for us to use and admire. Our goal is to promote this legacies that our ancestors left so that more people will be able to appreciate these legacies and to raise awareness that these legacies that we inherited are important for us so we must start to take care of these and preserve these legacies. By doing what we can all together, we will be able to preserve our cultural heritage so not only more people of this generation will be able to appreciate and admire these cultural heritages, but more people from the future generation will also be able to admire these cultural legacies because ‘It’s More Fun In the Philippines’.
